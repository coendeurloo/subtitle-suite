# Subtitle Suite - Beta 0.9.2 (Kodi.tv forum update)

Hi all,

Beta 0.9.2 is now available.

### What changed

- Manual Smart Sync now applies synced subtitles directly to playback (single or dual).
- Fixed a bug where playback could stay on one subtitle (for example English reference) after manual Smart Sync.
- Smart Sync subtitle selection now filters to the current episode in season folders (less risk of choosing wrong episode files).
- Menu label updated to `Smart Sync Subtitles (Manual)...`.
- Manual Smart Sync now closes subtitle/menu overlays and ends with one clear completion popup.

### Versioning

- Beta label: `0.9.2`
- Kodi addon version: `2.9.4`

Please keep sharing logs and edge cases. They directly drive these fixes.
